Koala

Koalas sleep up to 20 hours a day.

  • Scientific Name: Phascolarctos cinereus
  • Lifestyle: sedentary
  • Social Behaviour: solitary
  • Lifespan: 13 to 18 years

The Koala is an arboreal herbivorous marsupial native to Australia. It is easily recognisable by its stout, tailless body and large head with round, fluffy ears and large, spoon-shaped nose, with fur colour ranging from silver grey to chocolate brown. It has a body length of 60–85 cm (24–33 in) and a weight of 4–15 kg (9–33 lb), making it among the largest arboreal marsupials.
